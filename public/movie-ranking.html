The Best Movies According to Christine

My top 3 movies of all time

Armageddon

This has been my favourite movie of a time. I watched it first back in college. I like it since I watched it because the portrayal of Bruce Willis as a father is very touching.

Miracle In Cell No. 7

It is a Korean Movie about a Dad under the spectrum who was falsely accused of killing the daughter of a powerful person. He has as daughter and they don't have other family member. The Dad's co-inmates helped them so the daughter gets to enter their cell. It has a very sad ending because the Dad was sentenced to death.

Step Up All In

It is my go to feel good movie. It's just a chill musical movie with lots of dancing.
